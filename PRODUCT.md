# Product Context: Ekhon News (এখন নিউজ)

## Core Information
- **Name**: Ekhon News (এখন নিউজ)
- **Register**: brand
- **Mission**: To provide professional, trustworthy, and real-time business and general news to the people of Bangladesh.
- **Tagline**: Professional Business Television (প্রফেশনাল বিজনেস টেলিভিশন)

## Target Audience
- **Primary**: Business professionals, economy enthusiasts, and general news consumers in Bangladesh.
- **Secondary**: Bengali-speaking diaspora looking for reliable news from home.

## Tone & Voice
- **Professional**: Authoritative and factual.
- **Modern**: Forward-looking, leveraging clean and sophisticated design.
- **Trustworthy**: Transparent and reliable.

## Visual Direction (Initial)
- **Primary Color**: Amber/Gold (#F4A261) representing energy and value.
- **Secondary Color**: Deep Navy (#0B132B) for stability and professionalism.
- **Typography**: Clean Bengali serifs for headings (Tiro Bangla) and legible sans-serifs for body (Hind Siliguri).
